Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Siren ("we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Siren prediction-market trading and execution service at onsiren.xyz.

Google user data (Sign in with Google)

If you sign in to Siren with Google, we receive and process the following Google user data through our authentication provider (Privy) and Google OAuth:

  • What we collect: Your Google account email address, display name, and profile picture.
  • How we use it: To authenticate you, personalize your experience, and provide the Siren service (prediction markets, tokens, trading). We do not use Google user data for purposes other than providing or improving Siren.
  • With whom we share: We share this data with Privy (authentication), and infrastructure providers (e.g. hosting, email). We do not sell Google user data. These providers process data under their own terms.
  • Data protection: We use industry-standard security measures. Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel.
  • Retention and deletion: We retain Google user data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion by contacting us or disconnecting your Google account from Siren.

Information we collect

  • Account and login info: when you log in, we receive an identifier from your login provider (wallet address and/or OAuth provider user ID). If you sign in with Google/GitHub/X, we may receive basic profile information such as a display name or username.
  • Waitlist info: if you join the waitlist, we collect your email and optional name/wallet.
  • Usage and device info: basic diagnostics such as timestamps, pages viewed, and coarse location signals (e.g. country) for compliance and abuse prevention.
  • On-chain data: public blockchain data associated with wallet addresses you use with the Service.

How we use information

  • Provide and secure the Service (auth, access gating, fraud prevention).
  • Send onboarding and access-code emails when requested.
  • Operate features that require third-party APIs (quotes, swaps, market data).
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements, including jurisdiction-based restrictions.

How we share information

We share information with service providers to run Siren, such as: authentication (Privy), email delivery (Resend), data and infrastructure providers (e.g. RPC providers like Helius), and trading/quote providers (e.g. DFlow, Jupiter, Polymarket). These providers process information under their own terms and policies. We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention and deletion

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the Service and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data (including Google user data and waitlist information) by contacting us.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Service after updates become effective indicates acceptance.
